# Freeze

With this preset you can freeze a player for a while.

```yaml
settings:
  time: 10
  alert: "actionbar"
```

- Preset ID is `freeze`

### Settings

- `time` - Integer in seconds.
- `alert` - String for now only `bossbar` and `actionbar`
