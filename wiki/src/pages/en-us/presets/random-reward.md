# Random reward

With this preset you can randomizer other rewards.

```yaml
settings:
  rewards:
    - reward-1
    - reward-2
    - reward-3
```

- Preset ID is `random-reward`

### Settings

- `rewards` - List of Strings, they should simply have the id of the reward.
